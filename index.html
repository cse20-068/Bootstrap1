Manchester United are still the top club in Manchester, despite everything the Red Devils' crosstown rivals have accomplished in recent years. Manchester City have won five league titles in the last decade, with Man Utd lifting the Premier League trophy just once in that same timespan. But, despite City's recent success, Solskjaer said ahead of Saturday's derby that his side still is the top dog in Manchester, and will remain so in years to come.
